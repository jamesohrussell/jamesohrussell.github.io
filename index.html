James Russell

I currently work for The COMET Program at the University Corporation for Atmospheric Research (UCAR).

​At the COMET Program, I research and develop expertise in earth-system science topics, gather and visualize meteorological and climate data sets, build training products for the earth-system science workforce, and manage a variety of program areas and individual projects.

Formerly, I was a research scientist at a number of different universities. There, I developed code, analyzed a variety of large weather and climate data sets, ran weather forecast models, managed a research group, and taught classes.
